Apple Watch SE

A great deal to love.

Three stylish case colors. Powerful sensors for insights about your health and fitness. Innovative safety features. Convenient ways to stay connected.
A faster dual-core processor for added performance. Apple Watch SE is feature packed, and now it’s a better value than ever.

Fast is fashionable.

Fast is fashionable.

Apple Watch SE is available in three case colors: Midnight, Starlight, and Silver. And the new S8 SiP makes it up to 20% faster,1 giving it the power to do more.

Redesigned with the planet in mind.

Redesigned with the planet in mind.

The redesigned back case is made with a new production process that reduces its carbon emissions over 80%. It’s perfectly color-matched to the watch case, which is made with 100% recycled aluminum.

Swim-suitable.

Swim-suitable.

Apple Watch SE is water resistant 50 meters.2 Track your splits and sets in the pool. Map your route in open water. See accurate metrics even when using a kickboard. Whether you’re splashing, swimming, or surfing, the water’s fine.

More metrics.
More motivation.

More metrics. More motivation.

Stay active and motivated with Activity rings. And train like never before with the enhanced Workout app. Now you can try custom workouts and get advanced fitness metrics like Heart Rate Zones, Stride Length, Ground Contact Time, and Vertical Oscillation.

Take
apple fitness+
further.

Take Apple Fitness+ further with real-time personalized metrics.

Experience real-time metrics from Apple Watch in every workout. Choose from 11 workout types and a library of over 3000 workouts in 4K Ultra HD. Get new workouts every week, from 5 to 45 minutes. Focus on your wellbeing with guided meditations. Take audio-guided walks and runs with just your watch.

Get 3 months free when you buy an Apple Watch.3

Crash Detection. A safety feature we hope you'll never need.

For the first time, Apple Watch SE is able to detect if you’re in a severe car crash. When a crash is detected, Crash Detection can automatically connect you with emergency services, provide dispatchers with your location, and notify your emergency contacts.4 Crash Detection uses the combined power of an improved 3-axis gyroscope and a new g-force accelerometer that has the highest dynamic range in any smartwatch. It also uses a microphone, a barometer, GPS, and an advanced sensor-fusion algorithm trained on over 1 million hours of real-world crash data to detect if you’re in a severe crash.

Watches out for your heart health.

Watches out for your heart health.

Check your heart rate at any time with the Heart Rate app. And get notifications for high or low heart rate or an irregular rhythm.5

Stay centered

with Mindfulness.

Stay centered with Mindfulness.

The Mindfulness app is here to help you reduce everyday stress, be more present, and mentally prepare to take on new challenges. It can lead you through a series of calming breaths. And remind you to center yourself by reflecting on a short, thought-provoking theme. If you’re an Apple Fitness+ subscriber, you can practice mindfulness with audio-guided meditations anywhere you go.

Get a closer look at your shut-eye.

Get a closer look at your shut-eye.

The Sleep app tracks your sleep, working with your iPhone to help you create a schedule and bedtime routine to meet your sleep goals. See how much time you spent in different sleep stages like REM, Core, or Deep sleep, as well as when you might have woken up. View your sleep trends to see how consistently you’re meeting your goals. The only thing it won’t do is count sheep.

More connected,
far and away.

More connected far and away.

Take a call from the trails. Send a text from the beach. Check notifications with a glance. With available cellular and international roaming,6 you can do more, even when you don’t have your phone, in more places than ever.7

Family Setup. Your family joined at the wrist.

Family Setup

One iPhone
makes everyone's Apple Watch tick.

With Family Setup, everyone in the family, like your kids and older adults, can have an Apple Watch.8 Even those who don’t have their own iPhone yet. It’s all the connectivity, wellness, and safety features you love, for everyone you love. Fun and independence for them. Peace of mind for you.

Pairing is
caring.

Family Setup lets you pair watches for your children and older family members to your iPhone. Everyone gets their own phone number, and you can manage each watch using the Apple Watch app on your iPhone.

A great call

for kids.

With Family Setup, kids get to experience many of the capabilities of Apple Watch. They can stay in touch with the people you approve, by calling and texting. And have fun streaming music, downloading apps, and paying for stuff with Apple Cash Family. You can also schedule Schooltime to limit Apple Watch features when they need to focus. Even the Activity rings have been rethought to better reflect how they exercise — tracking Move minutes instead of active calories. And with location sharing, you’ll get alerts when kids get home or aren’t where they’re expected to be.

A smart call for
older adults.

Apple Watch can open up a whole new world for older family members. They get the same connectivity benefits of Apple Watch, like calling, texting, and location sharing to stay close to the family. And if they need it, they can make the text larger or bolded.

Safety features.

Apple Watch comes with important safety features like Fall Detection which can detect if your loved ones have taken a hard fall. If it senses they're immobile, Emergency SOS will connect them with emergency services — even if they haven't signed up for a cellular plan. They can also get help in an emergency — all over the world by simply pressing and holding the side button.

Health features.

Apple Watch can alert family members if it detects unusually high or low heart rates, as well as measure their current heart rate. And all the Activity goals, including Stand hours and Exercise minutes, can be customized to a level that works for older family members.

Family Setup. Your family joined at the wrist.

With Family Setup, everyone in the family, like your kids and older adults, can have an Apple Watch. Even those who don’t yet have their own iPhone.8 It’s all the connectivity, wellness, and safety features you love, for everyone you love. Fun and independence for them. Peace of mind for you.

Apps to make it yours.

Apps to make it yours.

Expand what your watch can do with tens of thousands of apps from the App Store. Discover apps for just about everything, from working on your tennis serve to tracking your bike rides to navigating the subway. You’ll also find Apple Watch apps for many of your favorite iPhone apps.

Let’s hear
it for music, podcasts, and audiobooks.

Let's hear it for music, podcasts, and audiobooks.

Apple Music gives you millions of tracks of musical motivation.9 You can also catch up on the latest podcasts or listen to an audiobook. It’s all the inspiration you need for your workouts. And all the entertainment you need for your rest days.

Swap styles in a snap.

Swap styles in a snap.

Apple Watch bands are designed to be interchangeable in just a few seconds. With a wide assortment of styles and colors, including Nike bands, you can change your look in no time.

Put your best face forward.

Put your best face forward.

Choose from dozens of Apple-designed watch faces, and new faces from Nike. And customize your own faces with complications, colors, and styles tailored to whatever you’re into. You can even share faces with friends and family by email or text.

Even more to discover.

Wallet.

Send money to friends10 or pay for just about anything with Apple Pay.11 And use Wallet as your ID, home key, car key, or boarding pass.12

Siri.

Do almost everything on Apple Watch — ask for directions, get a weather report, or play a song — just by talking to Siri.

Find Devices. Find People. Find Items.

Locate devices or get pinged when you leave your iPhone behind. See where your friends are. Track down an AirTag. Do it all from your watch.

Noise.

The Noise app lets you know when high noise levels around you can possibly affect your hearing.

Cycle Tracking.

Log your period, record symptoms like cramps, and get predictions for your next fertile window.13

Home.

Control lights, a thermostat, and the garage door. Or access a security camera to see who’s at the front door.

Camera Remote.

Use Apple Watch as a viewfinder for your iPhone camera. See a preview, then set a timer or just snap away.

Maps.

Search for locations and get turn-by-turn directions from Maps right on your wrist. You can even receive a tap when it’s time to turn.

Medications.

With the Medications app, you can get convenient and discreet reminders so you can quickly log medications.14

Environment

We take responsibility for the environmental footprint of our products throughout their life cycle. We’re committed to one day sourcing 100% recycled and renewable materials across all of our products and packaging. Apple Watch is designed with a list of features to reduce environmental impact.
